About

Muchlis In English, a Punk Rock quartet from Bandung that was formed in 2020. Fronted by
Zaki Asshiddiq (Vocal), Dechan Martawidjaja (Bass), Alvin Petyrs (Guitar), and Viqcux (Drums). in 2021,
it has released a mini album entitled "Nerdy Punk" and then a single "Menerka Jawaban dari
Bayangan, Keabadian" in October 2022, in May 2023 has realeased movie video from single "KRAIT".
